How it was made

There appears to be to contradicting statements concerning the entropy of field post letters. On one hand they have been the center of plenty research in fields such as Alltagsgeschichte (every day history) and literary studies. On the other hand, scholars such as Berghan (1969) and Fussell (1989) were outspoken critics of using field post letters for research. Fussel described them as merely serving the purpose of sending signs of life to loved ones. This hints at field post letters being formulaic and redundant. So what is it? README.
